Our Story

From The Shop Floor To The Show Field

RAD Motor Group was built by people who never lost sight of where they came from — and never stopped chasing what's possible.

Who We Are

Roots We're Proud Of

RAD Motor Group started with a wrench, a workspace and a refusal to cut corners. Years of honest industrial, collision and mechanical work earned us a reputation for getting the job done right — and that hard-earned credibility became our foundation.

As our skills grew, so did our ambition. We began taking on the builds most shops wouldn't touch: vintage restorations, exotic refinishes and full custom creations. Today, that same crew delivers concours-worthy work without ever losing the grit that started it all.

One group, two divisions, one standard — the kind of work we'd be proud to put our name on.
